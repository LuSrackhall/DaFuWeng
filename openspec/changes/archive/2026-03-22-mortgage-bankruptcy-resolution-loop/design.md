## Context

The deficit state is only useful if the room can resolve it. The minimum recovery baseline is to let the player mortgage property for cash or declare bankruptcy and leave the turn loop.

## Decisions

### 1. Mortgage is a direct command on owned property
Only the current deficit player can mortgage one of their unmortgaged properties.

### 2. Mortgaged property stops collecting rent
This keeps the economy coherent and creates a visible tradeoff for recovery.

### 3. Bankruptcy is minimal and authoritative
The bankrupt player is marked out of play, their properties are cleared, and the room advances to the next active player.
